<h3>Vision</h3>
<p>Every student inspired to connect with and love their Mother Tongue.</p>
<h3>Mission</h3>
<p>To equip students with strong language skills and a lifelong love for
their Mother Tongue.</p>
<h3>Key Programmes</h3>
<h4>MTL Programme</h4>
<p>The MTL fortnight is organised to create an immersive environment for
the learning of MTL through a range of activities, competitions and workshops.
Through active participation, students are able to experience MTL as a
"living language".</p>

<h4>Learning Journey</h4>
<p>Learning Journeys are organised to give students an opportunity to experience
a more in-depth appreciation of the MTL culture through drama and/or performances.
The programme provides an authentic experience beyond the classroom.</p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Sec_3_CL_LJ.jpg">

<h4>Stretch Programme</h4>
<p>Students are identified to represent the school in various competitions,
attend workshops and act as ambassadors in our partnership with external
stakeholders. In 2025, a group of 24 Malay students, led by 4 Malay teachers,
participated in our inaugural TIE trip to Kuala Lumpur, where they deepened
their understanding of new cultures and engaged in authentic learning experiences
within a Malay-speaking environment.</p>
