<h3>Vision</h3>
<p>To nurture empathetic communicators, discerning readers and creative inquirers.</p>
<h3>Mission</h3>
<p>To empower students to connect with diverse perspectives, critically engage
with texts, and express themselves with authenticity and flair.</p>
<h3>KEY PROGRAMMES&nbsp;</h3>
<h5>As Learners...</h5>
<p><strong>English Language</strong>
</p>
<p><em>English Language Trail</em>&nbsp;</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>experiential learning to make grammar and language appreciation come alive&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>self-directed research on possible language games&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>adapt them into their proposal writing exercise</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>This programme provides an&nbsp;<strong>authentic learning experience</strong>&nbsp;for
students to see how the learning process can create a real-life impact
to their lives.</p>
<p><strong>Literature in English</strong>
</p>
<p><em>Passion Arts Festival</em>&nbsp;where the following are displayed
throughout the year:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>graphic novels (Secondary 1)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>comics (Secondary 2)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>shape poems (Secondary 3)&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>anthology of poems titled ‘My Father’ (Secondary 4)</p>
</li>
</ul>

<h5>As Thinkers...</h5>
<p><strong>English Language</strong>
</p>
<p><em>NewsMaker</em>&nbsp;<em>Programme</em>&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>integrating receptive and productive language skills where students hone
their communicative&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;
&nbsp;competencies&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>challenge students to develop self-awareness and appreciation of wider
social issues</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>allows students to consider deeply their opinions&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>develop an awareness of how best to position what they say in order to
win over their audience</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>encourages them to express themselves confidently</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Literature in English</strong>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>ensure students engage critically with the texts and contexts they are
exposed to hone their&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;
&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;appreciation of understanding Character, Theme, Plot,
Setting and Style</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>bring alive their multiple graphic, comic and video projects</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>partnership&nbsp;with the NLB for the re-opening of the Tampines Regional
Library at Our Tampines Hub</p>
</li>
</ul>
