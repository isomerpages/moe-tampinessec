<h3>Vision &amp; Mission</h3>
<p>To nurture students to become confident problem solvers by equipping them
with the essential mathematical skills and knowledge through problem solving.</p>

<h3>Key Programmes</h3>
<h4>Secondary 1</h4>
<p><strong>NIE Mathematics Challenge</strong>
<br>Students take part in the 90 minutes national online Mathematics Quiz
to receive personalized feedback on their Mathematical problem solving
abilities.</p>
<h4>Secondary 2</h4>
<p><strong>Alge-Tournament</strong> 
<br>Students play games specially designed to apply their knowledge and skills
in algebra manipulation.</p>
<p><strong>Singapore Mathematics Olympiad Competition (Junior Category)</strong>
<br>An annual national competition to test and stretch the ingenuity of our
high-ability students in mathematical problem solving.</p>
