<h3>Vision</h3>
<p>Empowering every student to be a confident and critical thinker through
problem solving in Mathematics.</p>
<p></p>
<h3>Mission</h3>
<p>To nurture mathematical thinkers by:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Delivering engaging and rigorous lessons aligned with the Singapore curriculum.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Fostering problem-solving, reasoning, and communication skills.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Supporting diverse learners to achieve their fullest potential.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Cultivating a growth mindset and appreciation for the relevance of mathematics
in everyday life.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h3>Key Programmes</h3>
<h4>Secondary 1</h4>
<p><strong>NIE Mathematics Challenge</strong>
</p>
<p>Students take part in the 90 minutes national online Mathematics Quiz
to receive personalized feedback on their Mathematical problem solving
abilities.</p>
<h4>Secondary 2</h4>
<p><strong>Alge-Tournament</strong>
</p>
<p>Students play games specially designed to apply their knowledge and skills
in algebra manipulation.</p>
<p><strong>Singapore Mathematics Olympiad Competition (Junior Category)</strong>
</p>
<p>An annual national competition to test and stretch the ingenuity of our
high-ability students in mathematical problem solving.</p>
<h4>Secondary 3</h4>
<p><strong>Financial Literacy Programme</strong>
</p>
<p>A programme to develop and enhance our students' financial literacy.</p>
<h4>Secondary 4</h4>
<p><strong>Singapore Mathematics Olympiad</strong>
</p>
<p>An annual national competition to test and stretch the ingenuity of our
high-ability students in mathematical problem solving.</p>
