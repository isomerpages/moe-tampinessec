<p>On the 9th and 12th of April, Tampines Secondary School proudly commemorated
her 38th Annual Awards Day and Speech Day respectively. These significant
events served as platforms to honour both academic and non-academic achievements,
while providing opportunities for students to shine. This year's theme
'Growing Stronger Together, Excelling in Every Endeavour' embodies the
collective spirit and determination of the Tampinesian community. It underscores
the significance of unity and collaboration in pursuing excellence across
all facets of school life. By fostering a culture of support and encouragement,
Tampinesians can leverage their combined strengths to overcome challenges
and achieve success together.</p>
<p></p>

</p>
<p>The Awards Day ceremony on April 9th was a moment of pride as more than
150 Tampinesians were recognised for their outstanding performances in
academics and character. Students received prestigious accolades such as
'Top in Subject', 'First in Class', and 'ICARE' awards. Parents, teachers,
and staff gathered to celebrate the achievements of these deserving students,
acknowledging their hard work and dedication.</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Speech Day festivities commenced on April 12th with a grand parade
square event, graced by the esteemed presence of Mr. Suhaimi bin Rafdi,
Director of Special Projects at People's Association and our School Advisory
Committee Chairman. The Uniform Group contingents marched with precision,
accompanied by the harmonious melodies of the Concert Band. The multi-purpose
hall then hosted the awards presentation. Among the award recipients were
Nurul Illyanis Binte Muhammad Khairul, who received the Tampinesian of
the Year Award, Sharifah Nur Amanina Binte Syed Mohamad Shahab, who received
the Sportsperson of the Year Award, and Myra Irysha Mohd Izam, who received
the Artiste of the Year Award. These pinnacle awards highlighted the winners'
pursuit of excellence and served as an inspiration to others.</p>
<p>Moreover, we honored the contributions of our dedicated staff, supportive
parents, and esteemed alumni to our vibrant school community. The Speech
Day celebration brimmed with joy and gratitude as we recognized the commitment
demonstrated by each award recipient. The event featured captivating performances
by our talented Modern Chinese Dance, Malay Dance, Show Choir, Dance Dynamics,
Concert Band, and DramaInk, showcasing the diversity and creativity within
our school.</p>
<p>As we reflect on the Annual Awards Day and Speech Day celebrations, we
see not just milestones, but affirmations of our school's ethos. These
events underscore our dedication to fostering a culture of excellence,
unity, and inclusivity. With each passing year, we renew our commitment
to nurturing the holistic development of our students, preparing them to
embrace challenges with resilience and determination. As we move forward,
we strive to uphold these values, ensuring that every Tampinesian is empowered
to reach their fullest potential and contribute positively to our community
and beyond.</p>
