<p>Dear 4E &amp; 5N Tampinesians,</p>
<p>The results of the 2025 Singapore-Cambridge General Certificate of Education
Ordinary Level (GCE O-Level) Examination will be released on 14 January,
2.00pm.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Your parents/guardians may accompany you to school for the results.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Reporting Venue: School Hall (Please be seated by 2.00 pm)</p>
<p></p>
<p>Attire: Full/Half School Uniform (House PE T shirt properly tucked in)
&amp; Appropriate hair style/colour ( no dyed/coloured hair allowed)</p>
<p></p>
<p>Please refer to the MOE <a href="https://www.moe.gov.sg/news/press-releases/20260107-release-of-2025-singapore-cambridge-gce-o-level-examination-results-and-2026-joint-admissions-exercise" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank"><u>website</u></a> for
more details on the Joint Admissions Exercise (JAE), as well as the WhatsApp
update from your FTs for more information should you be unwell/unable to
collect your results.&nbsp;</p>
<p>
<br>On behalf of the Tampinesian community, we wish you all the best!</p>
