<p>Dear 4NA/NT Tampinesians,</p>
<p>The results of the 2025 Singapore-Cambridge General Certificate of Education
Normal (Academic)-[N(A)] and Normal (Technical)-[N(T)] Level Examinations
will be released this Thursday, 18 December 2025, 2.00pm.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Your parents/guardians may accompany you to school for the results.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Reporting Venue: School Hall (Please be seated by 2.00 pm)</p>
<p></p>
<p>Attire: Full/Half School Uniform (House PE T shirt properly tucked in)
&amp; Appropriate hair style/colour ( no dyed/coloured hair allowed)</p>
<p></p>
<p>Please refer to the MOE <a href="https://www.moe.gov.sg/news/press-releases/20251211-release-of-2025-singapore-cambridge-gce-na-nt-level-examination-results-on-18-december-2025" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank"><u>website</u></a> for
more details on the application for DPP, PFP and ITE courses, as well as
the WhatsApp update from your FTs for more information should you be unwell/unable
to collect your results.&nbsp;</p>
<p>
<br>On behalf of the Tampinesian community, we wish you all the best!</p>
