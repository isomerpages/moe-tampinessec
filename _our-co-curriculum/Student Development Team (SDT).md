<h3>Mission</h3>
<p>The SDT seeks to build a culture of care in which every student is engaged
in learning and is motivated to give their best.</p>
<h3>Goals</h3>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>designs and implements processes, programmes and structures to promote
and support&nbsp;a whole-school approach to student development and student
well-being for all students.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>provides support and intervention for specific groups of students who
require additional help, including students with special educational needs.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>works closely with all Instructional and non-Instructional Programme Departments
to develop students to be confident and resilient learners and leaders
through the following strategies:</p>
</li>
</ol>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>student empowerment in school experiences</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>intentional reinforcement across multiple contexts in total curriculum</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>consistent messaging in school and at home by adult role-models</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>supportive networks with peers and adults</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>positive behaviour and management of challenging or bullying behaviour</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>early intervention through identification and referral for students who
need additional help.</p>
</li>
</ul>
